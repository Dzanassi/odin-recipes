Mouth watering Steak & Lobster Combo


for this recipe you will need:


  1. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  2. 2 teaspoons plus 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  3. 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
  4. 3 tablespoons chopped green onions
  5. 2 tablespoons minced fresh thyme
  6. 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
  7. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  9. 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  10. 1/2 cup butter, softened
  11. 1 cup soft bread crumbs
  12. 1/4 cup butter, melted
  13. 4 lobster tails (8 to 10 ounces each)
  14. 4 beef tenderloin steaks (4 ounces each)
  15. 4 teaspoons coarsely ground pepper



Step by Step Directions:

  1. In a small skillet, saute garlic in 2 teaspoons oil until tender; remove from the heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the parsley, green onions, thyme, lemon zest, salt, pepper, cayenne and reserved garlic.
  3. Set aside 1/2 cup for crumb topping.
  4. Add softened butter to remaining herb mixture; mix well.
  5. Shape into a 1-in.-thick log; wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes or until firm.
  6. For crumb topping, combine the bread crumbs, melted butter and reserved herb mixture; set aside.
  7. Preheat oven to 375째. Split lobster tails in half lengthwise. With cut side up and using scissors, cut along the edge of shell to loosen the cartilage covering the tail meat from the shell; remove and discard cartilage.
  8. Place lobster tails on a baking sheet; top with reserved crumb topping. Bake, uncovered, until meat is firm and opaque and crumbs are golden brown, 15-20 minutes.
  9. Meanwhile, sprinkle steaks with coarse pepper. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook steaks in remaining oil until meat reaches desired doneness, 4-5 minutes on each side (for medium-rare, a thermometer should read 135째; medium, 140째; medium-well, 145째).
  10. Unwrap herb butter; cut four 1/4-in. slices from log. Place 1 slice on each steak. Serve with lobster. Rewrap remaining butter; refrigerate for 1 week or freeze for up to 3 months.
